Moſt Strange and VVonderful News from a place call'd the LEISTER, By Caſtle-Street, in the Pariſh of St. Martins in the Fields: Of a moſt Dreadful Diſcovery of a Murther of a Man and a Child, made on Sonday Morning being the 11th inſtant, on a Dunghill there; of the finding the Skin, and Nails of a Mans Hands, with his Privy-Members, Heart, Liver, Breaſt, and great Quantities of the Fleſh of his Buttocks, &c. alſo the Hart, Liver, Hand, &c. of a Child, with the ſtrange Manner of the Diſcovery by a Butcher, and one John Williams a Labouring Man.
